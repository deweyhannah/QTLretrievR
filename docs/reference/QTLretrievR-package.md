# QTLretrievR: Calculate the Map, Peaks, Mediation, and Effects for Quantitative Trait Loci

An integration of 'qtl2' and 'intermediate' to map genotypes to probes
(genoprobs), calculate Quantitative Trait Loci (QTL) peaks, identify
mediators of the peaks, and identify the phenotypic effects within
experimental data from diversity outbred (DO) mice.
